# Professional Resume Templates

This directory contains comprehensive, visually appealing professional templates for each distinct profile type. Each template is specifically designed to highlight the unique strengths and characteristics of different career stages and professional backgrounds.

## Template Overview

### 1. Modern Professional Template (`ModernTemplatePDF.js`)
- **Profile Types**: Universal (all profiles)
- **Design**: Clean, modern, minimalist
- **Best For**: General professional use across all industries
- **Features**: ATS-optimized, customizable colors, responsive layout
- **Accent Color**: Blue (#2563eb)

### 2. Student & Graduate Template (`StudentTemplatePDF.js`)
- **Profile Types**: Student
- **Design**: Fresh, optimistic, education-focused
- **Best For**: Recent graduates, current students, entry-level positions
- **Features**: 
  - Education section prominence
  - GPA highlighting (if 3.5+)
  - Academic projects integration
  - Leadership activities showcase
  - Awards and honors display
- **Accent Color**: Blue (#3B82F6)
- **Special Sections**: Academic Projects, Leadership & Activities

### 3. Executive Professional Template (`ExperiencedTemplatePDF.js`)
- **Profile Types**: Experienced
- **Design**: Sophisticated, authoritative, achievement-focused
- **Best For**: Senior professionals, executives, managers
- **Features**:
  - Leadership experience highlighting
  - Career progression timeline
  - Achievement metrics emphasis
  - Industry expertise showcase
  - Professional certifications
- **Accent Color**: Slate (#1E293B)
- **Special Sections**: Key Achievements, Career Progression, Certifications

### 4. Career Transition Template (`CareerChangerTemplatePDF.js`)
- **Profile Types**: Career Changer
- **Design**: Growth-oriented, transition-focused
- **Best For**: Professionals changing careers or industries
- **Features**:
  - Transferable skills emphasis
  - Career change motivation
  - New field preparation showcase
  - Goal alignment display
  - Industry transition visualization
- **Accent Color**: Green (#10B981)
- **Special Sections**: Transferable Skills, New Field Preparation, Career Goals

### 5. Academic & Research Template (`AcademicTemplatePDF.js`)
- **Profile Types**: Academic
- **Design**: Scholarly, research-focused, publication-heavy
- **Best For**: Researchers, professors, PhD candidates, academic professionals
- **Features**:
  - Publications list
  - Grant and funding tracking
  - Conference presentations
  - Research collaboration highlights
  - Teaching experience
  - Technical skills emphasis
- **Accent Color**: Purple (#7C3AED)
- **Special Sections**: Publications, Grants & Funding, Conference Presentations, Research Skills

### 6. Creative Professional Template (`CreativeTemplatePDF.js`)
- **Profile Types**: Creative
- **Design**: Visually appealing, portfolio-focused, artistic
- **Best For**: Designers, artists, writers, creative professionals
- **Features**:
  - Portfolio integration
  - Visual project showcase
  - Creative process highlighting
  - Client type diversity
  - Business skills balance
  - Industry involvement
- **Accent Color**: Orange (#F59E0B)
- **Special Sections**: Portfolio & Work Samples, Notable Projects, Creative Process, Industry Involvement

## Usage Guidelines

### Template Selection
1. **Identify Profile Type**: Choose based on career stage and background
2. **Consider Industry**: Match template style to industry expectations
3. **Evaluate Content**: Select template that best showcases your strengths
4. **Review Features**: Ensure template includes relevant sections

### Customization Best Practices
1. **Color Selection**: Choose colors that match your industry and personal brand
2. **Font Choice**: Select fonts appropriate for your field and target audience
3. **Section Order**: Prioritize sections that showcase your strongest qualifications
4. **Content Density**: Adjust font size based on content volume

### Quality Assurance
- Preview templates with both sample and personal data
- Test different customization options
- Ensure all sections render correctly
- Verify ATS compatibility
- Check professional appearance across different devices
