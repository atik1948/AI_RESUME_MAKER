# AI Resume Builder - User Experience Improvements

## Overview
This document outlines the comprehensive improvements made to enhance the AI Resume Builder's user experience and ensure all necessary information is collected for generating impactful CVs.

## Key Improvements Implemented

### 1. Enhanced Contact Information Collection
- **Phone Number**: Now marked as required field
- **Location**: Added as a required field (City & State/Country)
- **GitHub Profile**: Added as an optional field for technical professionals
- **Better Validation**: Enhanced form validation to ensure all required contact fields are completed
- **Improved User Guidance**: Added comprehensive tips and examples for each field

### 2. Enhanced Onboarding Process
- **Target Industry**: Added as a required field to better understand user's career focus
- **Career Goals**: Added as an optional but recommended field to collect user aspirations
- **Better Profile Type Selection**: Enhanced descriptions and guidance for each profile type

### 3. Improved Experience Form
- **Key Metrics & Achievements**: Added a dedicated field for quantifiable results and business impact
- **Better Placeholder Text**: Enhanced examples and guidance for writing effective job descriptions
- **Required Field Indicators**: Clear marking of required vs. optional fields

### 4. Enhanced Skills Collection
- **Skill Proficiency Levels**: Added Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced, and Expert levels
- **Skill Categories**: Technical, Languages, Soft Skills, and Tools
- **Better Skill Suggestions**: AI-powered skill recommendations based on education and experience
- **Visual Skill Display**: Enhanced skill tags with proficiency indicators

### 5. Improved Form Validation
- **Comprehensive Validation**: Enhanced validation for all critical fields
- **Better Error Messages**: More specific error messages guiding users on what needs to be completed
- **Step-by-Step Validation**: Each step now properly validates required information before allowing progression

### 6. Enhanced AI Content Generation
- **Better Context**: AI now receives comprehensive information about user's background
- **Profile-Specific Prompts**: Tailored AI prompts based on user's profile type and career goals
- **Enhanced Summary Generation**: Professional summaries now include career goals, target industries, and key skills

## Required Fields for AI Success

### Essential Information (Required)
1. **Full Name** - For personal identification
2. **Target Job Title** - For role-specific content generation
3. **Profile Type** - For tailored resume structure
4. **Target Industry** - For industry-specific language and focus
5. **Email** - For contact information
6. **Phone** - For contact information
7. **Location** - For geographic targeting
8. **Education** - At least one education entry with school and degree
9. **Work Experience** - At least one experience with company, job title, and description
10. **Skills** - At least one skill for resume content

### Recommended Information (Optional but Highly Valuable)
1. **Career Goals** - Helps AI generate more targeted content
2. **LinkedIn Profile** - Professional networking and credibility
3. **Portfolio/Website** - Showcases work and projects
4. **GitHub Profile** - Technical skills demonstration
5. **Relevant Coursework** - Academic background relevance
6. **Key Metrics & Achievements** - Quantifiable results for impact
7. **Skill Proficiency Levels** - Skill depth and expertise
8. **Academic Projects** - Student and recent graduate relevance
9. **Certifications** - Professional development and expertise
10. **Leadership Experience** - Management and leadership capabilities
